03 : About me

I have been teaching math for over 17 years moulding young minds to love math and enrich their thought process. I currently teach math to kids from grade 7 to 10. I use lots of animations along with the traditional whiteboard to keep kids completely engaged in the learning process and I conduct regular tests to make sure that kids know where they stand.

I consider myself a 'forever student' eager to both build on my academic foundations in math and technology and stay in tune with the latest trends through updation and adaptation. My hunger for knowledge and determination to turn information into action has helped me guide many students to achieve their full potential.

I believe mindfulness in the workplace is the key to success - a tenet I live out through my interests in travel, tennis, exercising, coding and passion for animation based teaching. Reach out to email@kayzad to connect.
